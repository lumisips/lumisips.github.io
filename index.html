👑 Why LumiSips Exists

LumiSips started in Anaheim, California with a simple goal: create a beverage brand built around identity, community feedback, and flavors people actually want.

Instead of guessing what customers want, the community helps shape every future release. Every vote, every flavor idea, and every interaction helps build LumiSips.
